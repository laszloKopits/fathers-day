The Rectangle Artists

Seven painters who built whole careers out of squares and rectangles. Every artwork on this page is generated live in your browser, in each artist's style — so you can hit "generate" forever and learn to recognize what makes each one tick.

Generated compositions are original, recreated programmatically in each artist's style. Real artworks shown are public domain (via Wikimedia Commons); works still under copyright are linked to museum/gallery sources rather than reproduced. Educational use. Built by daimon.
